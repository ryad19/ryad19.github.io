Game Restrictions

1- you can drop your disc in the top of each column
2- The player who forms a horizontal, vertical, or diagonal line of four of their colored discs wins the Game
3- If all of the columns are filled and no player has formed a line of four, then the game is a tie.
4- A player cannot play a disc in a column that is already full.
5- A player cannot remove their own disc from the board once it has been played.
6- Player Red start first
